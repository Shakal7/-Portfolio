Obstacle Avoiding Robot

An Obstacle Avoiding Robot is an autonomous device equipped with sensors to detect obstacles in its path. Upon sensing an obstacle, it utilizes predefined algorithms to navigate around it, ensuring continuous movement towards its intended destination. This technology finds applications in various fields, including robotics, automated guided vehicles (AGVs), and unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs), enhancing efficiency and safety in navigation tasks.
